In a single particle universe the particle’s mass can’t be measured, and other things can’t be measured such as time. Grapevine

Update: See: Grapevine for reference implementation and Hacker News. Grapevine is a distributed protocol for replacing twitter like systems. It is composed of nodes that add a proof of work over a message and nonces from previous nodes, so the proof of works stack up as messages pass through nodes. Messages with a higher total cost have a higher probability of being forwarded to the next node. Individual nodes decide the cost of the proof of work they add to the message.
